About This Website

This platform was developed with a simple goal in mind — to support FINKI students in navigating the often overwhelming process of finding internships. With technology advancing rapidly, hands-on experience is crucial, and we aim to bridge the gap between education and opportunity.

The website automatically scrapes two main sources: 1) Internship listings posted on the official FINKI website from partnered companies, and 2) Junior-level and internship positions from LinkedIn. The results are displayed in a clear, user-friendly way to help students make informed choices.

The site updates automatically to provide fresh listings and reduce the need for manual job searching. Every opportunity shown is meant to be relevant, accessible, and student-focused.

Built as a side project by a fellow FINKI student, this tool is open-source, lightweight, and designed to grow with your feedback. Thank you for using it — and good luck on your career journey!
